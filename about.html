For more than three years, BDLS has been providing language and capacity building services in Burkina Faso and internationally, focusing on competence, excellence and innovation to make a difference in the market. To date, we have succeeded in building the loyalty of several local and foreign organisations, which we regularly support in the implementation of their vision.

To work as a pivotal organization in Burkina Faso and progressively in the other francophone countries of West Africa, to inspire profound innovations in the areas of language services and capacity building, through the deployment of cutting-edge initiatives and technologies that connect people to opportunities and anticipate the ever-changing needs of modern organizations.

To revolutionize in Burkina Faso and in the French-speaking countries of the West African sub-region, the sectors of language services and capacity building, relying on tested skills and experience, to effectively support the performance of teams, organizations, private companies and public structures fighting for the Africa of tomorrow.

We are working to impact 1 million lives in Burkina Faso by 2024, through programs, projects and initiatives that equip young local leaders and professionals with strong English language skills and knowledge, connecting them and opening doors to the countless opportunities in the English-speaking world.
