Language Services

Only 7% of human communication is verbal. But what we say and how we say it has a decisive importance on our lives and the success of our businesses.

Content Translation & Conference Interpretation

We offer you the cream of the crop in professional content translation and conference interpreting services in English, French and Portuguese. Flexibility and efficiency are combined with confidentiality and respect for deadlines, to offer you the best in the industry today!

Creative and professional content writing

We are masters in the art of word handling! We know how to use their extraordinary power to give meaning and life to your ideas. Building your brand image and communicating your vision to your target audience are now as simple with us as ordering a pizza at your favorite restaurant.

Content proofreading and rewriting

Your ideas deserve to be expressed in the appropriate language and format to effectively achieve your goals. From the selective choice of appropriate phrases and expressions, to grammar and spelling checks, we work to make your content more lively, digestible and punchy.

Transcription of audio and video content
With the ever-increasing development of digital technology, multimedia content is now commonplace in many organizations. BDLS makes your life easier by taking care of transforming them accurately into editable and more easily manipulated text.

Video subtitling

Would you like to reach speakers of other languages with your videos, or simply help your team or partners better understand a video that is important to your organization? Now you have the solution at your fingertips with our video subtitling department.

English courses in the workplace

Because you and we know that ignoring English is a luxury you can no longer afford, BDLS comes to your home to make your team speak the language. Choose the days, hours and frequency of learning that suits you and we will effectively lead your staff to the result!

Capacity building services

Because we understand that professional success is very closely related to practical and leadership skills, we don't skimp on ways to lead you to a better version of yourself. 

Training courses in leadership and personal development  for professionals

The performance of teams and organisations is largely guaranteed by leaders who are deeply aware of their potential and who have mastered the art of positive influence. Our practical training courses, adapted to the local context, help your professionals achieve this level of excellence.


Training courses in management and entrepreneurship for company managers

With the panoply of new startups being created every year, addressing the need for capacity building for entrepreneurs is not only a pressing necessity, but also a critical action from an economic point of view. BDLS accompanies business promoters towards success, whatever the current stage (birth, development, maturity) of their organization.

Trainings courses in leadership and management of Non Governmental Organizations (NGOs)

The role of NGOs in community development and the fight against poverty in our various African countries is well established. BDLS intends to put its know-how and experience to the benefit of these different structures to give them a new dynamism and equip their staff with all the skills to meet the current challenges of our communities.

Training courses in civic leadership and management of civil society organisations

Strengthening the gains of democracy is crucial to preserving peace and stability. A responsible civil society, aware of its role and sufficiently influential, is crucial to maintain this political and social balance. Our trainings help civil society actors to fully play this role by providing them with the knowledge and experience necessary for the balance of power and cohesion within communities.

Training courses in public leadership and management of local administrations 

Local authorities are the grassroots development poles par excellence of our various States. Entrusting their governance to executives who understand the issues at stake and have the skills to effectively conduct sustainable development policies is a necessity today. At BDLS, our trainers know how to identify the specific needs of public authorities and how to meet them in the right way.

Training courses in career management for professionals

What are the best decisions you can make today to help guide your career? How do you organize yourself today to achieve your career goals in the next 10 years? What are the opportunities available in your sector that you must take advantage of at all costs? All these questions and many more can be answered through our training modules under this heading.
