Our action process

5 steps tested to provide you with top-quality language services!

Assignment

After validation of your order, we entrust it to the most experienced team of professionals within our services for the type of work you request.

Achievement

Our team works on your project taking into account the agreed delivery times and the specific technical aspects of your order.

Correction

A second independent team of proofreaders checks and corrects the initial work done by the first team and proposes improvements to the latter.

Approval

A final team of senior professionals carries out a thorough review of the work and proposes final improvements during a validation meeting.

Delivery

The final work validated by our management is delivered to you within the agreed deadlines and the execution team is at your disposal for any comments.
