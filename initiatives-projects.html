Initiatives and projects

We understand that our mission to significantly impact 1 million lives in Burkina Faso by 2024 will be a mere utopia if we do not take bold initiatives to bring it to life and increase our impact.

"English for Women Empowerment" is a project of "Blue Diamond Leading Solutions" whose goal is to empower, over a period of four (4) years, 1000 young women from all regions of Burkina Faso, by providing them with a solid foundation in English and by rigorously supporting their training to obtain an international certification in English language teaching. These young women will thus easily have employment or self-employment doors opened to them. More importantly, they will play a vital role as agents of change in each of their regions, helping us to implement a project with a greater social impact, which consists of the creation of more than 250 English clubs throughout Burkina Faso by 2024.

Visit the website of the programme

"English for Youth Empowerment" is an initiative that aims to improve young people's access to a wide variety of online opportunities (learning, employment, professional development, etc.) for which a good command of English is necessary. The project recruits each year in Ouagadougou, through a rigorous selection process (online application form - written test - oral test - final interview), fifty (50) young graduate students and professionals who are already at an intermediate level in English. Each cohort is offered the unique opportunity to significantly improve their level of English, through a six (06) month intensive learning program. In addition, throughout the program, all resources and information (useful websites, organizations, important dates, etc.) on online opportunities available to the youth are provided to them to increase their chances of success.

Visit the website of the programme

"English for Kids Summer Camp” is an initiative of "Blue Diamond Leading Solutions" which aims to offer parents in the city of Ouagadougou, a fun and educational environment in which their children are exposed to the practice of English in real life. This programme, which brings together 50 girls and boys aged 6 to 14 for two weeks at each edition, implements a series of playful, recreational and discovery activities, coupled with English language lessons, which deeply activate the communication skills of the participating children.

Visit the website of the programme

"BDLS English Club" is a crucible for exchange and free learning of the English language which is a powerful tool for capacity building of young executives, students and professionals in Burkina Faso. Our ambition through the activities of this club is to enable the less privileged strata of society to have access to quality education that prepares them for the challenges of the professional world and gives them enough options to be competitive in the job market and in the ruthless world of business.

"BDLS 1-minute-video series" is an initiative through which we leverage the power of short animated videos to help our subscribers acquire a solid knowledge of English. From vocabulary and grammar to idioms and conversational techniques, we make learning English dynamic and stimulating through these high quality videos.

"Weekly Inspiration 101" is a motivational tool aimed at encouraging a positive attitude at work and boosting the personal development and competitiveness of our various subscribers. We work to select powerful, topical English-language quotes, which we highlight with modern, attractive graphic designs that engage our readers and increase their commitment to success.

See previous posts

"English/French Equivalent Proverbs" is a program of "Blue Diamond Leading Solutions" whose goal is to allow French speakers each week to discover in English the proverbs corresponding to those they are familiar with and similarly English speakers to discover in French the proverbs corresponding to those they usually use. The objective is to allow these two categories of speakers to learn a second language (English or French) based on something as fascinating as proverbs.  

See previous posts

"9999 Expressions Françaises" is an initiative of "Blue Diamond Leading Solutions" aiming to highlight these idiomatic expressions that make up the richness of the French language. Each week, our team publishes at least one idiomatic expression with details on its origin and examples of its use in different contexts. This program allows the English-speaking community of our subscribers to deepen their knowledge of French while significantly improving their communication skills.
